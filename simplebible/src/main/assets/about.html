Simple Bible intends to
- Be easy to use.
- Avoid unnecessary features.
Keeping this in mind, I began this project; since it was something the elders I knew wanted in their Bible application. This is my attempt to meet their needs.

To contact me regarding suggestions, feedback or anything else, you can use the "Provide Feedback" option present under Settings within the application. This is an Open Source application and is located at the GitHub location pointed below.
GitHub  |  Google+
